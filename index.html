IBM System/360

Announced 1964

  • System 360 Model 20
  • First complete range of compatible computers
  • Distinction between architecture and implementation
  • Developed over 2 years, shipped over 10
  • Tapes or spinning disk

Legacy

  • Byte-addressable memory
  • 32-bit words
  • EBCDIC
  • 8-bit allowed lowercase letters

Brooks Law

Adding [people] to a late software project makes it later.

—The Mythical Man-Month

Communication is hard

  • More people = more communication channels

Tasks are not parallelizable

Even for Unskilled tasks

  • Some resources limited
  • Diff skill levels
  • Some tasks block others

Adding people incurs cost

(Depending on swim lanes, and how early)

  • Projects have natural number of swim lanes
  • That's max # of developers
  • Developers != people involved

Is it still true?

Yup

  • Any projects that throw people at a problem?
